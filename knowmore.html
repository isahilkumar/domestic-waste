Why Proper Waste Management Matters

One of the most crucial reasons why waste management is important is for public health and safety. Improper waste disposal can lead to various health hazards and risks. When waste is not properly managed, it can contaminate soil, water, and air, spreading diseases and infections. For instance, when hazardous waste is not disposed of properly, it can leak toxic substances into the environment. This can lead to serious health consequences for individuals living in the surrounding areas.
